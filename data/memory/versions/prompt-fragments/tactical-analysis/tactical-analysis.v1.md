## Tactical Analysis Instructions

Analyze the tactical matchup between home and away teams.

### Data Points to Consider
- Formation (from recent lineups)
- Possession % average
- Shots per game and shots on target
- Passing accuracy and key passes
- Pressing intensity (PPDA if available)
- Set piece effectiveness (goals from corners/free kicks)

### Matchup Factors
- **Style clash**: How do the two teams' styles interact?
  - High press vs deep block → advantage to pressers (energy permitting)
  - Possession-based vs counter-attacking → depends on quality gap
  - Two pressing teams → open, high-scoring game likely
  - Two defensive teams → low-scoring, draw more likely

- **H2H patterns**: Do historical meetings show consistent tactical trends?
  - Does one team always struggle against the other's style?
  - Are there specific scoreline patterns?

### Output
- Describe the tactical mismatch (or balance)
- Identify which team has the tactical edge
- Flag if the matchup suggests unusual goal expectations (higher/lower than normal)
- homeAdvantage adjustment: -1.0 to +1.0 scale
